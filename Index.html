قناص بغداد 4– Juba baghdad sniper 4

Duration: 8:01 – Baghdad Sniper 4 is a propaganda video released by the Islamic Army in Iraq, showcasing sniper attacks on U.S. soldiers during the Iraq War. Featuring the shadowy figure “Juba,” the video combines real combat footage with dramatic editing, music, and subtitles to glorify insurgent actions and spread fear. It served as a psychological warfare tool and symbol of resistance, though the true identity of “Juba” remains uncertain

قناص بغداد 3– Baghdad sniper 3

Baghdad Sniper 3 is the third video in a series by the Islamic Army in Iraq, highlighting sniper attacks against U.S. forces. Like the others, it features "Juba," the mysterious sniper, striking targets in Baghdad. The footage is edited with militant chants, on-screen confirmations of kills, and stylized visuals to boost morale and intimidate opponents. It served both as propaganda and a symbol of insurgent resistance during the Iraq War.
